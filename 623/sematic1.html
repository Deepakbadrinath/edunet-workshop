About Honda bike

article


The 2024 Honda Hornet 2.0 is a sporty and agile motorcycle ideal for both city commuting and weekend rides. It is powered by a 184.4 cc air-cooled, single-cylinder, 4-stroke SI engine that produces a maximum power of 16.99 PS at 8500 rpm and a peak torque of 15.7 Nm at 6000 rpm. The bike offers impressive mileage, with a claimed fuel efficiency of around 57.35 kmpl, making it a fuel-efficient option for daily use. The Hornet 2.0 boasts aggressive styling with a sharp tank design, a sporty split seat, and LED lighting all around. It features a fully digital 4.2-inch TFT instrument cluster that includes a gear position indicator, tachometer, trip meters, and a clock. Its suspension setup includes a golden upside-down (USD) front fork and a mono-shock rear suspension, which together provide excellent stability, handling, and ride comfort. In terms of safety, the Hornet 2.0 is equipped with dual-channel ABS, disc brakes front and rear, hazard lights, an engine kill switch, and Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC) for enhanced traction and control
